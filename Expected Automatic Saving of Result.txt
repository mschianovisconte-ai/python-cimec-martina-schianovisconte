Expected Automatic Saving of Results
The following files for sholl analysis are automatically saved inside Results/[filename]/:
Skeleton Analysis Outputs:

SA_Results_[filename].csv (Results table)
SA_Branch_Info_[filename].csv (Branch information)
[filename]-1-labeled-skeleton.tif
[filename]_Tagged-Skeleton.tif

Sholl Analysis Outputs:
Sholl_[filename].csv
Sholl_[filename].tif

Region of Interest (ROI) Image:
[filename]-ROI.tif (This image remains open after analysis.)
 
Example folder structure:
Results/
   ├── 7856-1/
   │   ├── SA_Results_7856-1.csv
   │   ├── SA_Branch_Info_7856-1.csv
   │   ├── 7856-1-labeled-skeleton.tif
   │   ├── 7856-1_Tagged-Skeleton.tif
   │   ├── Sholl_7856-1.csv
   │   ├── Sholl_7856-1.tif
   │   ├── 7856-1-ROI.tif

The following files will be saved automatically in a Counting Results folder:

Masked Image (_masked.tif) showing detected microglia
ROI File (_rois.zip) containing the refined ROIs
Measurement Results (_results.csv) with area and count data
